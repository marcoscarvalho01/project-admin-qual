PROJECT OVERVIEW

Welcome to the Midas Qualification Exam! The purpose of this project is to create prompt-answer pairs along with self-contained, traceable supporting facts and rationales based on SEC filings.

GROUND RULES

  • NO LLMs: No external AI is allowed to assist with any portion of this task.
  • No PII: Do not include any personally identifiable information of ordinary employees or private individuals.
